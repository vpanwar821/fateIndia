First time in India, an accessory product that will ensure the security of your vehicle through Fingerprint or by using a PIN.

About the product

  • Fate introduces its first product that is one of its kind, it will take the security of your car to a whole new level.You can restrict your vehicle's rights to yourself or whoever you want.
  • Car Knight will enable only authenticated users to start the car by having user's fingerprint impressions saved. You can also unlock the ignition system with a customised digital PIN. It consists of a Fingerprint sensor, a keypad and display screen.
  • This will add a layer of security to the vehicle leaving thieves and unauthorised people very disappointed.

Features This can be implemented well in the cars that are already being used as well as in the new cars you are planning to buy.

  • Needs Authentication (FingerPrint or PIN) to Start the vehicle
  • Set your own 4-digit PIN
  • Change the PIN whenever desired
  • Add users with the help of PIN
  • 360 degree fingerprint recognition
  • Delete users with the help of PIN
  • Forgot PIN option, in case of emergency
  • Valet mode option to give the vehicle for Valet service or to someone who is unauthorized.
  • Fingerprint recognition response time close to 1 second.
  • Detachable device for user convenience
